Add your consultation notes

Your artificial intelligence assistant will review these notes and use them to produce an initial draft assessment report.

Provide a clear summary of the claimant’s health conditions, including onset, duration, progression and any recent changes.

List all prescribed and over‑the‑counter medications, including dosage, frequency and any reported side effects.

Describe the claimant’s living situation, daily routines, work history and any social or environmental factors that affect their functioning.

Record any difficulties with planning, preparing, or safely cooking meals.

Describe issues with eating, drinking, or managing nutritional needs independently.

Note the claimant’s ability to follow treatment plans, manage therapy routines, or carry out medical interventions.

Document any challenges with personal hygiene tasks, including washing, bathing or using facilities safely.

Describe continence issues or difficulties accessing, using, or managing toilet needs.

Record problems with selecting clothing, dressing independently, or removing garments.

Note speech, comprehension, articulation or communication difficulties observed during the consultation.

Describe any challenges with reading, processing written information or understanding visual prompts.

Record how the claimant engages in face‑to‑face interactions, including cooperation, rapport, and social communication skills.

Describe any limitations in managing money, budgeting tasks or making financial decisions.

Document difficulties with route planning, navigation, or travelling independently.

Record mobility issues, including walking distances, balance, pain, stability or the use of mobility aids.

Describe any observations about the claimant’s mood, behaviour, communication, awareness, and interaction during the assessment.

Record any informal observations you noticed before, during, or after the assessment, not captured elsewhere.
